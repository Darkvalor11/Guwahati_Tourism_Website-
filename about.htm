About Us

Hello! My name is Washique, and I am the creator of the Guwahati Tourism Website. My goal was to build a resourceful, visually engaging website to introduce visitors to the vibrant culture, food, nightlife, and attractions of Guwahati.

How This Website Was Created

The development process involved ideation, research, designing the user interface, and writing code for each unique page. Every section was made with care to highlight the experiences of Guwahati city. The technologies used include HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
