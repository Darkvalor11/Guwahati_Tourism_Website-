Getting There: Guwahati is well connected by road, rail, and air. Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport links to major Indian cities. The railway station is a hub for Northeast India rail routes.

Accommodation Types: Choose from luxury hotels, cozy guesthouses, and nature resorts depending on your budget and preference.

Local Highlights: Explore the iconic Kamakhya Temple, enjoy the scenic Brahmaputra riverfront, and visit local markets for Assamese culture and cuisine.

Tips: Book accommodations in advance during festival seasons. Opt for centrally located stays to reduce travel time to popular attractions.
