Things to Do in Guwahati

  • Srimanta Sankaradeva Kalakshetra
    A cultural hub comprising a museum, library & open-air theater with mountain views.
  • Nehru Park
    Scenic landscaped park with flower gardens and sculptures, ideal for family visits.
  • ISKCON Guwahati
    A tranquil Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna known for its architecture.
  • Science Museum, Khanapara
    Interactive science exhibits including an aquarium and playground for kids.
  • Assam State Museum
    Displays artifacts like sculptures, coins, and historical books reflecting regional heritage.
  • Umananda Temple
    Historic Shiva temple located on Umananda Island, accessed via ferry ride.
  • Purva Tirupati Sri Balaji Temple
    Famous for South Indian architecture and impressive stone idols in serene grounds.
  • Shraddhanjali Kanan
    Popular park with illuminations, playgrounds, green spaces perfect for relaxing outdoors.

Guwahati City life

Experience the vibrant nightlife of Guwahati, where lively music venues, cozy cafes, and bustling night markets bring the city to life after dusk.

  • GS road Area
    Popular for bars, pubs, and restaurants with live music and great ambiance.
  • Uzan Bazaar
    Famous for evening food markets and street snacks, offering authentic local flavors.
  • Lokur Park
    A charming park where evening cultural events and concerts take place.
  • Guwahati Club
    Exclusive nightlife spot with social events, music, and dining in a sophisticated setup.
